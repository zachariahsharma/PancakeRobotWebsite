What are the Pancake Robots?

The Pancake Robots are a team of middle schoolers in First Lego League. We compete, create, and learn through robotics and innovative ideas. We are playful and love to have fun while working. We are all best friends and use that to power our team. We use discovery and innovation to code and build our robot, and use inclusion and impact to create a innovation project. All of us are hardworking and make sure we love what we do.
